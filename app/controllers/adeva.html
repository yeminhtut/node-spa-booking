Frequently Asked Questions

What if I’m late to my session? 
Please arrive as close to your check-in window as possible. If you’re late, we may not be able to hold your session for you and you may have to reserve another one.

Can I change or cancel my session? 
If you need to cancel your session, click or tap on the link “Manage your session” above. You’ll be taken to a page where you can cancel your session. To change your session, just cancel your existing session and book a new one.
